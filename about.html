ABOUT US

"Everything started when I realized how fun and personalized cooking is. The fact that the same dish comes out with different flavors from different hands took me on a long journey in the mysterious world of flavors."

Chilli Pepper is the result of many flavorful information such as tricks, cooking techniques, the use of spices, the desire to create new flavors and so on. came into the world. The new flavors we discovered made our guests familiar faces, so now we all enjoy being members of a large family together.

Even hearing the words "than usual" from the mouth of our guests, who can't eat anything that their mother didn't make, gives us the determination to work harder. We are constantly trying… We are striving to find the best, the newest, the most delicious.

In return, we receive a smile on the faces of our guests who finish their meal. We also consider suggestions from our guests. We first share our new tastes with our guests and get their opinions. Because this is the home of our guests as well as ours… Chilli Pepper is a place they prefer not only to eat, but also to enjoy eating.!

If you also like to eat, Chilli Pepper.' We are waiting for you.
